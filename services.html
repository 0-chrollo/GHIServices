More Detail

Steel-based tiles with a stone-coated finish that looks like thatch — without the fire risk or maintenance. Can be installed directly over existing thatch.

  • Class A fire rating — reduces lightning risk
  • 88% lighter than concrete tiles
  • Weatherproof, pest-proof and insect-resistant
  • Available in multiple natural stone-coated colours
  • Can be applied over old thatch or new structures
  • No re-thatching required ever again
  • 20-year warranty included

We convert your thatch to Chromadek or zinc sheeting — either by over-sheeting or full reconstruction, depending on the condition of your existing structure.

  • Over-sheeting option — no need to vacate the property
  • Full rebuild available — old thatch stripped and structure reinforced
  • New insulation installed to replace thatch's thermal performance
  • Eliminates fire risk and re-thatching costs permanently
  • Zinc and Chromadek last 80–100 years
  • Existing structure used where possible to reduce costs

A non-toxic, water-based fire retardant applied by high-pressure spray. We are certified applicators — the certificate issued is recognised for insurance purposes.

  • Slows flame spread significantly
  • Protects against UV, fungi, rot and pests
  • Extends the life of your thatch
  • Safe and non-toxic for residents
  • New thatch: 5-year certificate
  • Old thatch: 4-year certificate
  • Reapplication every 3–5 years recommended

Full lightning protection systems installed for thatch and tile roofs. All work is certified and compliant, protecting your structure and electrical systems from strikes.

  • Air terminals placed at all roof high points
  • Copper or aluminium conductor cables
  • Ground rods buried at required depth
  • Surge protection for electrical systems
  • Servicing and maintenance of existing systems
  • Compliance certificate issued on completion
